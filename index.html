About Us

CREATE Incubation Centre

Confederation for Research, Entrepreneurship & Technology Enablement (CREATE) was established in January 2021 to foster research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It actively supports startups and entrepreneurs through incubation programs, seminars, conferences, and workshops.
Dedicated to empowering emerging entrepreneurs, CREATE serves as a strong foundation for nurturing and advancing cutting-edge startups.

OUR MISSION

To create entrepreneurial opportunities for students, faculty and researchers and to nurture technology and knowledge based ventures through their start-up phase by providing the necessary support

OUR VISION

To be a center of excellence that fosters synergy between the academia and the industry that fuels entrepreneurial spirit and inculcates research bent of mind among students to help them to be self reliant and contribute to the nation building.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to help create and grow young businesses by providing them with necessary support and financial and technical services.

INCHARGE'S MESSAGE

Incubation Center, IIIT Lucknow is a platform for young entrepreneurs who are passionate to create and innovate. The objective is to cultivate and enrich the spirit of entrepreneurship among students to build up profitable and sustainable businesses with novel ideas and practices. This center provides mentorship, infrastructure, network and finance to nurture entrepreneurial initiatives of the students and take the innovation ecosystem to greater heights.
